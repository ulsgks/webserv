The motherfucking philosophy

Let's cut the crap. A good website:

  • Loads fucking fast without JavaScript bloat or massive images
  • Works on any screen size without horizontal scrolling bullshit
  • Looks the same in every browser
  • Won't blind you at night thanks to automatic dark theme detection
  • Is accessible to everyone and readable with proper contrast

This site isn't just satire, it's a manifesto. Remember when websites were made to convey information? Not to track your every move, harvest your data, or force you to sign up for a fucking newsletter?
